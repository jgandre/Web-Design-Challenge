Summary: Impact of Latitude on Weather


Welcome to the Latitude vs Weather: the Analysis! The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. To accomplish this analysis, we pulled data from the OpenWeatherAPI to assemble a data set of 500 random cities.

After assembling the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot various aspects of the weather vs. latitude. The factors we looked at include: temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity.
